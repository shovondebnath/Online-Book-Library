Our Story

DigiShelf was founded in 2024 with one simple dream: make the world's greatest books available to everyone, instantly, beautifully, and affordably.

Frustrated by scattered e-book platforms, high prices, and clunky apps, we decided to build something different — a digital bookshelf that feels like home.

Today, hundreds of thousands of readers across the globe trust DigiShelf to discover, read, and collect books they love.
